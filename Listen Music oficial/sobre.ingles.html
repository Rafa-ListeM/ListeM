About Us

Mission

Our mission is to bring comfort and welcome to those who need or like to have music present in their lives, in your most precious moments the right music can create memories. We hope to be able to satisfy you at all times, whether during cleaning, during studies, during your nights of light sleep or on the road, whether in sad or happy moments, because we are a family, we are LinteM, always here with and for you! (^-^)

Vision

We want to become one of the best and most popular music hosting and download sites, with the best plans and excellent quality of service, always looking for and willing to improve and thus better serve and satisfy our public. Our main objective is to offer our hosting and download services with comfort, quality, security and practical and easy-to-understand access for all.

Values

As our values, we take respect for both the client and the artists and composers, empathy with the public for better satisfaction, we also carry with us solidarity and ethics.
